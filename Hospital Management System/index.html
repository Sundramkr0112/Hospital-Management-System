WELCOME TO GLAH

A state-of-the art modern facility in the heart of Mathura, Uttar Pradesh, India, it is spread over 10 acres and has a built-up area of over 440000 square feet. Currently the hospital has 300+ beds with a capacity to expand to 400 beds.

GLAH Hospital is a tertiary care flagship unit providing advanced medical services including Cardiac Sciences, Neuro Sciences, Orthopaedics, Cancer Care, Emergency Medicine and more.

GLAH Hospital provides holistic healthcare that includes prevention, treatment, rehabilitation and health education for patients and their families.
